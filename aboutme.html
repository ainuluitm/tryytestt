VIDEOGRAPHY.

I like recording videos and editing them to form a whole new video that could convey a story. Mostly, I record videos in the form of vlog. I have a Youtube Channel where I post vlogs, mainly containing travel or concert contents. It is also where I post fan-taken videos at a concert, for the purpose of sharing first-hand experiences of mine. Usually, I record moments for keepsake but there are times when I share them publicly.

I usually record videos with my phone or my SONY WX500 camera. I believe one can start making videos with just anything that has a camera! To edit them, I use Wondershare Filmora which is an amazing video-editing software that is easy to use and has pretty much everything an amateur editor like me would need.



PHOTOGRAPHY.

In every pictures taken, there is always a specific moment or meaning behind them. Other than scenery and food pictures, I also capture just any random photos that I feel would be memorable to look back at. It is always fun to reminisce those moments.

Recently, I'm interested in film photography but have only been able to use applications on my phone such as Gudak Cam and Filmlike, to have a film-kind photos. Would love to own a film camera when I finally learn more on how to use it, specifically the Kodak M35.

I also use Lightroom to edit most of my photos, and sometimes Adobe Photoshop to edit photos taken at concerts to edit out any background noise.


TRAVELLING.

Travelling is going from one place to another, and does not necessarily mean only going to countries abroad. Travelling is very eye-opening as one gets to learn more about the places they have never been to before.

As for me, other than travelling to other countries for vacation purposes, I also have travelled out for concerts. It is always a fun time getting to know more friends from other countries, learn more about their culture and also widen my network.
